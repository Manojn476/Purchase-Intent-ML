Will the user make a purchase?

Enter the following details to predict purchase intent

  • Product Related Pages: Number of product pages viewed by the visitor. (e.g., 5)
  • Product Related Duration: Time spent on product pages in seconds. (e.g., 120.5)
  • Bounce Rate: If a visitor leaves your site after viewing only one page, that's a bounce. Enter the percentage as a decimal (0.45 means 45% of visitors bounced). Lower is better!
  • Exit Rate: The percentage of visitors who leave your site from a specific page. Enter as a decimal (0.35 means 35% of visitors exited from this page). Lower is better!
  • Page Value: The average monetary value of the page in dollars. This represents how much revenue this page contributes to your business. (e.g., 10.50 means $10.50)
  • Month: Month of the visit, 0 for January to 11 for December. (e.g., 5)
  • Visitor Type: Type of visitor: Returning, New, or Other.
  • Weekend Visit: Whether the visit occurred on a weekend (Yes/No).
